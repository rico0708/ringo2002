About me/我　

Born in 2002/生于2002年

I was born in Kanagawa, Japan in 2002. When I was little, I loved to play both at home and outside. I really liked reading illustrated books, and the ones about the body were my favorite and I used to read them a lot. So my dream was to become a doctor. Many of my relatives are involved in the medical field, so I have longed to be a doctor.

我于2002年出生在日本神奈川。当我小的时候，我喜欢在家里和外面玩。我非常喜欢看绘本，关于身体的绘本是我的最爱，我曾经经常读它们。所以我小的时候的梦想是成为一名医生。我的许多亲戚都从事医学领域的工作，所以我从小就有这样的梦想

After I became a junior high school student/成为初中学生后

I spent six years of my life in junior high and high school at an all-girls school. My school focused on English education and international studies. Since I had a lot of exposure to English, I came to love it. As I studied international social issues in parallel, I gradually came to realize that I wanted to be a person who could be active in the world. I was a member of the dance club. I was also the head of the club. The friends I made in junior high and high school made it possible for me to spend the days of my youth. I am still good friends with them even now.

我的中学和高校生活有六年是在一所纯女校度过的。我的学校专注于英语教育和国际研究。我喜欢英语，因为我有很多机会接触到它。 当我同时研究国际社会问题时，我逐渐想成为一个能在世界上发挥积极作用的人。 我是舞蹈俱乐部的成员。我也是该俱乐部的负责人。我在初中和高中结识的朋友帮助我度过了我的青春。我和他们仍然非常亲近。
